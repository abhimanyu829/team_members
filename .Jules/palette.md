## 2024-05-02 - Form Accessibility Improvement\n**Learning:** Forms constructed using pure `<div>` structures with adjacent `<label>` and `<input>` elements frequently miss programatic linkage via `htmlFor` and `id` attributes. This prevents screen readers from properly announcing the label when the input is focused, and degrades usability by preventing users from clicking the label to focus the input.\n**Action:** Always ensure that form inputs have explicit `id` attributes, and that their corresponding `<label>` elements have an `htmlFor` attribute matching that `id`. This is especially important for dynamically generated forms.
